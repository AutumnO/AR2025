If youâ€™re flying into an airport other than ACV, we recommend renting a car to get you the rest of the way. The Amtrak does offer bus service to Fortuna and Eureka but you will need to figure out how to get to the departing station from the airport.
For those flying into ACV, your transportation options include car rentals, shuttles, cabs, and the city bus. Lyft and Uber are available but tend to have more issues picking up from ACV than the local cab services. Due to the small number of flights coming in to ACV you may find it easy to coordinate with other guests on transportation.

Local Transportation

If you are not driving to Humboldt County, we highly recommend renting a car. We are rural and most services will not be in walking distance from where you stay. There are many sightseeing opportunies that will make it worth the additional expense. See our guide to Humboldt County for some of these locations.
If renting a car is not an option for you, Lyft and Uber are available, as well as city cabs. While we don't have personal experience with local cabs, Supreme Rides is well rated and primarily serves Eureka. Redwood Transit Authority(RTA) is our city bus. It has a wide coverage area and is very resonably priced. Keep in mind it does not run on Sundays.
If you are unable to find transportation let us know. We can help connect you with someone else with transportation or find another solution.

Our Guide to Northern Humboldt County

This area is known for beautiful ocean views and towering redwoods. While we have recommended some of OUR favorite places to visit, anywhere you go you will likely find something to enjoy.
Our favorite places to enjoy nature
Avenue of the Giants - amazing scenic drive, multiple places to stop and walk around, see the redwoods up close.
Humboldt Bay National Wildlife Refuge - beautiful walk along the wetlands, accessible and pretty much entirely flat. fantastic wildlife viewing.
HumBoats - rent kayaks and boat around Humboldt Bay, try a double kayak to test the strength of your relationship!
Samoa Beach - beach access point with parking. great place to walk along the coastline. dress with layers, be prepared for wind!
Moonstone Beach - beautiful protected cove, less wind than Samoa. Fun bouldering to the north and river wading to the south. North of here is Luffenjoltz Beach which has great tidepooling.
Arcata Community Forest - hike through the redwood forest, choose trails that match the level of difficulty you're looking for. This was my first hike when I moved up to Humboldt County and still one of my favorites! Look to either Redwood Park or the two Sunny Brae Forest Trailheads for nearby parking.
Trinidad Head - beautiful ocean views, mostly paved, easy but relatively steep hike. Located in a beautiful town that is also worth checking out.
For Breakfast (or just coffee)
Fernbridge Cafe & Coffee Barn - Fernbridge - pricey but delicious, highly recommend the sweet potato fries and housemade ranch.
Trish's Out of the Way Cafe - Fortuna - diner style breakfast, great staff, our favorite pre-hike meal.
Ramone's Bakery & Cafe - Eureka (multiple locations) - our work lunch spot, both grab and go meals and menu options.
Dick Taylor Craft Chocolate - Eureka - amazing cafe, great chocolate. check out some of their magazines while you're there.
Our Favorite Fancy Date Spots
Double D Steak - Fortuna - unique and charming appeteaser plates, delicious steaks.
Diver Bar & Grill - Eureka - best charcuterie we've found so far, never a miss so far.
Brick & Fire Bistro - Eureka - some of the best food Humboldt County has to offer, reservations HIGHLY recommended.
Larrupin' Cafe - Trinidad - THE best food Humboldt County has to offer, reservations recommended.
Other Food & Drink Recommendations
Slice of Humboldt - Arcata - Autumn's favorite pie place, savory hand pies, pot pies, and sweet pies. if you find yourself in Arcata don't miss it!
Humboldt Cider Company - Eureka - Local cider brewery, high quality cider. you will often see food trucks nearby too.
Bob's Footlongs - Fortuna - fave lazy dinner, great hamburgers (try "The Chef").
Paul's Live From New York Pizza - Fortuna & Eureka - great slices, our second go-to for lazy nights.
Cap's Food Shack - Check the site for daily location - Riley's beloved gourmet In-N-Out alternative.
Other Events and Venues
Cafe Mokka - Arcata - private redwood hot tubs and saunas. show up at 11am sharp when they open to reserve up to 48 hours in advance. if you can't get a spot, it's a beautiful spot to enjoy a drink from their cafe!
Arcata Theatre Lounge - Arcata - old movie showings and live music. check their schedule to see what will be happening our wedding weekend!
Sequoia Park Zoo - Eureka - while the zoo is fairly small, their claim to fame is the Redwood Sky Walk. Walk along the redwood canopy more than 100 ft up!
Ferndale, CA - hometown of local legend Guy Fieri, Ferndale is a great place to see our historical victorian houses.
Old Town Eureka - another spot to see the old victorians, great window shopping, and a view of the harbor.
